About the Breeders

We are located in rural Virginia Beach. We live on over 6 1/2 acres of land so our dogs and puppies get plenty of fresh air and exercise. We all enjoy lots of nature and many beautiful sunsets. ​

Over 25 years we have occasionally bred our own personal dogs including; Bouviers, Corgis, Dobermans, and
Golden Retrievers. After raising our two boys with Dobermans, we discovered Standard Poodles. We loved their
intelligence, agility, and non-shedding. After some diligent research, we discovered GOLDENDOODLES.

​ We began breeding Goldendoodles in 2003. We kept a Goldendoodle from our first breeding.
She is the famous….Shenleigh O’Doodle! She has become an ambassador for the breed!

We have established ourselves a top reputation for producing smart, happy, healthy,
well-structured dogs with great temperaments and intelligence. We earned the distinction
of being listed as recommended breeders at the worldwide website Goldendoodles.com.

All About Goldendoodles

A Goldendoodle is a hybrid cross between a Golden Retriever and a Standard Poodle.
They are a fantastic mix of two amazing and intelligent dog breeds.
They are the perfect dog for many reasons:

  • They are very intelligent. The Standard Poodle is #2 on the dog smart scale & the Golden Retriever is #4.

  • They LOVE people as they were originally bred to be therapy dogs.

  • They are very easy to train as they want to please you.

  • They have a gorgeous coat ranging from curly to wavy. They look like shaggy retrievers!
    The coat is very low shedding and has no dander so you don’t get the “wet dog” smell and they do well with people with allergies.
